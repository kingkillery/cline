## Learnings
- ACP model enumeration depends on `providerModels` plus `getSessionModelState`, so adding `openai-codex` there is sufficient for client-visible model listing when that provider is selected.
- The `api_req_started` translator case is intentionally lightweight and exists to surface progress before the provider streams its first token.
- Default model resolution for providers without a provider-specific key still works through the generic `actModeApiModelId` and `planModeApiModelId` path.
